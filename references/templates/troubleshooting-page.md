## Lifecycle

Troubleshooting pages often benefit from review and supersession notes when fixes drift.

- Review status (`review_status`): draft | reviewed | needs_review | archived
- Last reviewed (`last_reviewed`): YYYY-MM-DD
- Retention class (`retention_class`): working | reference | historical | ephemeral
- Confidence basis (`confidence_basis`): why the suggested fix or diagnosis is trusted
- Supersedes / Superseded by (`supersedes`, `superseded_by`): use when a canonical fix replaces an older workaround
- Consolidation status (`consolidation_status`): raw | curated | crystallized
- Crystallized from (`crystallized_from`): incident notes, issue threads, or temporary workarounds consolidated here

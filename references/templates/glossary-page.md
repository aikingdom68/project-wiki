## Lifecycle

Glossary pages usually need only a small lifecycle note.

- Review status (`review_status`): draft | reviewed | needs_review | archived
- Last reviewed (`last_reviewed`): YYYY-MM-DD
- Retention class (`retention_class`): working | reference | historical | ephemeral
- Confidence basis (`confidence_basis`): why the terminology mapping is reliable
- Supersedes / Superseded by (`supersedes`, `superseded_by`): use when a term entry or glossary page replaces an older canonical wording
- Consolidation status (`consolidation_status`): raw | curated | crystallized
- Crystallized from (`crystallized_from`): source notes or scattered term lists consolidated here

## Lifecycle

Keep lifecycle notes short and decision-focused.

- Review status (`review_status`): draft | reviewed | needs_review | archived
- Last reviewed (`last_reviewed`): YYYY-MM-DD
- Retention class (`retention_class`): working | reference | historical | ephemeral
- Confidence basis (`confidence_basis`): why the current confidence is justified
- Supersedes / Superseded by (`supersedes`, `superseded_by`): use when this decision replaces an earlier recommendation or ADR
- Consolidation status (`consolidation_status`): raw | curated | crystallized
- Crystallized from (`crystallized_from`): comparison notes, evaluation output, or interim memos consolidated into this record
